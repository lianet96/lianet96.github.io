Hi, I’m Lianet

Cuban-American artist and filmmaker based in South Florida. My work blends tender, coquette aesthetics with sharp storytelling—film, illustration, and installations that explore identity, memory, and the strange poetry of everyday life.

Recent highlights include Pena Íntima (Chroma Art Film Festival) and Haunted Hours (10-min short). I love building worlds that feel soft to the touch but cut like glass.

  • Film direction & editing
  • 2D/3D character design
  • Mixed-media installations
  • Brand visuals & poster design
